How to Play Blackjack

Objective

Gather a sum of cards that is higher than the sum of the dealer's cards without going over 21.

Rules

  • Player and dealer are both dealt 2 cards each. One of the dealer's cards is placed face down.
  • Player then has the option to "Hit" or "Stand". If player decides to "Hit" another card is given to the player.
  • Player can "Hit" as many times as they want, so long as the sum of their cards is not over 21.
  • If the sum of the player's cards is over 21, player "Busts" and the game is over.
  • Once player "Stands" the dealer will play their cards until the dealer "Stands" or "Busts".
  • Player and dealer will then compare hands and the person with highest sum wins.
  • If both player and dealer reach 21 the game is tied.
  • Player can obtain a "Blackjack" by having a hand that comprises of an Ace and a card that has a value of 10.

Card Values

  • Number cards 2-10 are value on the card.
  • Jack, Queen, King are all valued at 10.
  • Ace can either be 1 or 11. Whichever is more advantageous.
